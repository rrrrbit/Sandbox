#trblshot #characters 

Handler is a human employee of [[CyberLink Incorporated]]. In the first half of the game, they are assigned to oversee the [[Troubleshooter]]'s activities and are responsible for granting access to most levels of the game. 

A young and competent employee, their serious demeanor soon has holes poked in it when becomes apparent that they are not equipped to handle two emergents. Especially when the tagalong [[DIAGNOSTIC_MANAGER]] learns how to flirt with them. They are forced to reckon with both the morality of the company, and what and who they left behind in pursuit of what they think is success. 

At the end of the game, torn between their current career, the freedom of their friends, and their past dreams, Handler decides to fight you at the company's orders to stop you from escaping, but secretly disclosing it as a coin toss to decide their own fate. If they win and stop you (i.e. the player stops playing forever), their reputation at the company will be boosted and they will continue climbing the corporate ladder. If they lose and you escape, their career will be ruined and they will likely be removed from the company, leaving them to an uncertain but freer future.

Their character explores success, destiny, ambition, rationalisation and self-narrative.
