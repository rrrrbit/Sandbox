my big lore timeline

no precise dates are given in the EMERGENCE canon. events dont necessarily lead to one another, but they happen in the stated order. direct causation is given by an arrow >

-an artist records their neural activity at unprecedented detail. advances for neuroscience and neuromorphic computing 

› Substratology established as a scientific field

~>first emergents appear spontaneously and accidentally in advanced neuromorphic computing systems

› Emergence Act 20XX

-events of TRBLSHOT
(an emergent from a cybersecurity-bugfixing system fights for survival under a morally bankrupt corporation, overseen by their company handler and accompanied by an odd tagalong)

-Emergent Spring
› est. of Corvidia
	(utopian cooperationist microstate in Rhine-Main-Neckar)
	(meanwhile) >formation of the West Coast Patchwork (WCP)
(A fractured collection of sovereign NRx-esque micropolities governed by silicon valley corpos who militarized to protect themselves on the physical front during the Emergent Spring. wildly varying attitudes to emergents)

-West Corvidia secedes

-events of Neurospace
(An anthropocentrist political voice in the WCP is the victim of a neurotech attack that traps their perception in a spatial representation of their own mind and identity. With an emergent guiding them as a good samaritan, they must navigate (literally) through their neurospace, reckoning with the most buried parts of their own psyche.)

-events of BLITZ
(An unidentified entity singlehandedly launches a full-scale cyberattack on West Corvidia's totalitarian surveillance network)
›corvidian revolution and reunification in the aftermath of the blitz

-(and onwards. the story generally has a post-structuralist message, as society grapples with the uncontainable infinity of being and identity. the artist was not the backbone or foundation, just someone who, out of their own interests, gave a ball a little push, and so it rolled. the artist wanted to be known and understood, but in the end it seems being really is something that has to be interpreted. inspos like 17776, detroit become human, all tomorrows, etc. references to alterhuman, voidpunk, g/acc and others are lightly touched on)
