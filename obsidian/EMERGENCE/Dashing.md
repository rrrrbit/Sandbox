Dashing is a movement mechanic in TRBLSHT. The default keybind to dash is shift.

## Dash techs

### Basic
These dash types and behaviours are hard-coded into the game.
#### Short Dash (S-Dash)
Tap the dash key for less than 0.125 seconds to perform a short and snappy dash.

#### Long Dash (L-Dash)
Hold the dash key after the dash period to keep most of the gained momentum.

#### Dash Bounce (D-Bounce)
If you hit a wall, floor or ceiling during the dash period you will bounce off of it and keep momentum.
